// The Role

What You’ll
Actually Do.

A clear, focused engineering role. Here’s the real scope of the work, no fluff.

01
📐
Residential Design & Analysis
Prepare structural designs, mark-ups, and preliminary layouts for residential projects to a high standard of accuracy. Produce structural analysis, calculations, and engineering documentation suitable for senior review. The technical work is the heart of the role.
02
🏗️
Site Inspections & Reports
Conduct site visits, structural inspections, construction compliance inspections, reinforcement inspections, compaction testing, and dilapidation surveys. Produce reports and certificates as required. You’ll be on-site regularly, not just at a desk.
03
📋
Project Workflow
Organise your assigned workflow with self-initiative, meeting timeframes and client expectations. Coordinate with drafters, builders, and consultants. Apply NCC, Australian Standards, and WA regulatory requirements. Escalate sensibly when matters fall outside your scope.
04
💬
Client Communication
Maintain client relationships on assigned jobs through effective, commercially aware communication. Respond to client requirements, update on progress, handle queries within scope, and escalate fee or scope matters to senior engineers as appropriate.
